Charity

Here at SoTSpace, we understand a large amount of waste coming from our rockets. That is why we decided to donate to charities that promote more renewable sources of energy. We take some and we give some back.

 

Click me if you want to know more about Renewable World.

We are funding an organization called Renewable World, to help developing nations by supplying them with clean energy. Five percent of our ticket sales go to funding charity. This organization specialises in developing and deploying effective ways of bringing off-grid renewable energy solutions to the most remote and underserved communities, empowering them to achieve sustainable and resilient lives. Clean, reliable energy is critical to education, water, health, agriculture, business, and more. Energy poverty is one of the primary barriers for families to realise their full potential. Since 2008, they have reached 40,000 people with renewable energy technologies and services. They aim to increase access to clean, affordable and sustainable energy for 100,000 people by 2022.

Carbon Dioxide utilisation is a challenging scientific task. That is why we are giving another five percent of our ticket sales, to fund research at the University of Toronto, on working on sustainable technology for breaking down C02 molecules to releasing Oxygen and bind Carbon atoms together.
