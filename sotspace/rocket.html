Our Mission

In 1958 the first manmade satellite was launched into space, marking the beginning of the space age. Then in 1969, Neil Armstrong stepped on the moon. It was a historic moment. Now we are going to set another landmark by being the first to send space tourists to the moon. We are the leaders of the space industry, the first to reach the historic landmarks in front of all humanity. Our mission is to be the first company that brings space tourism to the moon! Join us, and participate in this historic endeavour!
